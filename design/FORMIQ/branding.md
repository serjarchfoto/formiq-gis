# Branding

## Brand Character

FORMIQ is a professional GIS and architectural planning product.

The interface should feel:

- Precise
- Spatial
- Calm
- Architectural
- Modern
- Trustworthy

## Logo

Logo typography:

- General Sans Bold

Use the logo with enough air. Do not crowd it with controls.

## Visual Identity

- Primary brand color: `#229ED9`
- Main typography: Inter Variable
- Large background typography may use Inter Black with very low opacity.
- Avoid decorative illustration styles that make the product feel generic.
