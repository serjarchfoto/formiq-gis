# Branding

## Brand Character

FORMIQ should feel precise, spatial, calm, architectural, modern, and trustworthy.

## Logo

- Logo font: General Sans Bold
- Keep the logo clear of surrounding controls

## Voice

- Use concise, professional interface copy.
- Prefer direct labels.
- Avoid decorative or overly playful phrasing.

## Identity

- Primary brand color: `#229ED9`
- Main typography: Inter Variable
- Large background type may use Inter Black at very low opacity
